Egypt Air Refund Process Step By Step

Need assistance with bookings, cancellations, baggage, or flight changes? Here’s a complete guide to reaching EgyptAir via phone, email, live chat, and more.

📞 Call EgyptAir Directly (24/7 Hotline)

The fastest way to speak with a live representative is by calling EgyptAir’s main customer service line. Support is available 24/7, so you can call anytime.

  • U.S. Toll-Free: 1-800-EgyptAir (1-800-397-4827)
  • International: +1-212-581-5600
  • Egypt Domestic: 1717 (Mobile) or 090070000 (Landline)

Tip: Have your booking reference, SkyMiles number, and travel details ready for faster service.

Best for: Urgent booking changes, cancellations, flight delays, or immediate travel needs.

💬 Use EgyptAir Live Chat

If you prefer not to wait on hold, EgyptAir’s live chat is a great option. Access it through their official website or the Fly EgyptAir app.

Best for: Quick questions, minor booking adjustments, and when you can’t make a call.

📧 Email EgyptAir Customer Support

For non-urgent concerns or when you need to send documents (like refund requests or medical certificates), email is ideal.

  • General Support: customeraff@egyptair.com
  • Payments Support: paymentsupport@egyptair.com
  • Baggage Claims: mybaggage@egyptair.com

Response Time: Usually within a few business days.

Best for: Detailed inquiries, complaints, or documentation-heavy requests.

📱 Contact EgyptAir via Mobile App

The Fly EgyptAir app isn’t just for checking in. You can manage bookings, chat with support, and even request callbacks.

Best for: On-the-go support, managing reservations, and receiving real-time notifications.

🌐 Reach Out via Social Media

EgyptAir is active on platforms like Twitter and Facebook. Sending a direct message often gets a quick response, especially for public complaints or quick clarifications.

  • Twitter: @EGYPTAIR
  • Facebook: EGYPTAIR

Best for: Non-urgent issues, sharing feedback, or getting updates on widespread disruptions.

🏢 Visit an EgyptAir Customer Service Desk at the Airport

If you’re already at the airport and need immediate assistance—like rebooking after a cancellation—visit the EgyptAir service desk.

Best for: Last-minute changes, baggage issues, or special travel needs.

📞 Use EgyptAir’s Automated Phone System

If you don’t need a live agent, EgyptAir’s automated system can help you check flight status, baggage info, or basic booking details.

Best for: Flight status, automated check-in, or simple information requests.

🔁 Request a Callback from EgyptAir

Don’t want to wait on hold? Use the callback feature on EgyptAir’s website or app. Enter your phone number and issue; EgyptAir will call you back when an agent is available.

Best for: Busy travelers who don’t want to wait on hold.

🌍 Reach Out via EgyptAir International Support Numbers

Traveling abroad? EgyptAir has dedicated numbers for different countries. Always check the official website for the correct number in your region.

Best for: International travel support, local language assistance.

♿ Utilize EgyptAir Accessibility Support

If you need special assistance due to a disability or medical condition, EgyptAir offers dedicated support lines and services.

Best for: Wheelchair requests, medical accommodations, or traveling with service animals.

🔍 Visit EgyptAir Official Website for FAQs and Self-Service

Many issues can be resolved without contacting an agent. The EgyptAir website offers comprehensive FAQs, booking management tools, and travel advisories.
